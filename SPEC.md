# ZTL — Zero-Trust Logic

Specification v0.2 — 2026-07-11. Status: word-first phase closed, the
test bench works, the passport (preprint §10) is adopted: verdicts are
two-valued, Z is an input mark.

## The principle (one, generates everything)

**Truth is never granted on credit.** When the mark Z ("truth not
earned") on an input reaches any connective, it is read in all classical
ways at once, and the connective returns T only if T is forced under
**every** reading; otherwise F. Every occurrence of Z is substituted
independently; the result is always classical — Z evaporates at the
first touch of an operator (greedy collapse).

Formally: `op(...Z...) = ⋀ { op(...v...) : v ∈ {T,F} }` over each
Z-argument.

## Values and marks

Truth values: `T` (earned), `F` (falsehood) — verdicts are always
two-valued. Input mark: `Z` — zero-trust, "not earned"; a property of an
atomic datum, not a truth value (in the design dialogues of 2026-07-10
it appeared as "L"). The solver's service state `N` ("not yet
computed", present only under self-reference, never escapes outward) is
neither a value nor a mark. The lazy register reuses the symbol Z
positionally for this phase (the role differs, not the alphabet —
preprint §10: Kleene's third element conflates the mark and the phase
by making their shared symbol a value of assertions).

## Tables (generated by the principle; MEASURED ztl.py)

```
 ¬ : T→F, F→T, Z→F
 ∧ : the Z-row and Z-column are all F
 ∨ : Z∨T=T, otherwise every cell with Z is F
 → : Z→T=T, F→Z=T, every other cell with Z is F   (T→Z=F, Z→Z=F, Z→F=F)
 ⊕ : every cell with Z is F
 ↔ : every cell with Z is F
```

Anchor axiom cells (dictated at design time, reproduced by the
principle, checked by `tests_axioms()`): ¬Z=F, ¬¬Z=T, Z↔Z=F, Z↔T=F,
Z⊕Z=F, Z⊕F=F, Z⊕T=F.

## Fixed forks (decided; do not reopen without cause)

1. **¬Z = F, not "Z collapses to F at the atom".** Theorem: {¬Z=F,
   T→Z=F} are incompatible with contraposition. Contraposition was
   sacrificed deliberately; atomic collapse would turn ZTL verbatim
   into Bochvar's isomorph B3□ and erase all novelty.
2. **Greedy collapse, not lazy flow** (SQL NULL is the lazy relative:
   Z flows, falsehood once at the WHERE boundary). Greediness = the
   Tarski ladder in evaluation order: every operator over Z is a floor;
   ¬¬Z=T is a report about a report, not "laundering of truth".
3. **Z↔Z = F — quarantine is detectable**: isZ(x) = ¬(x↔x) is
   expressible inside the language. The price: the avenger liar μ is
   expressible too — "μ is false or isZ(μ)"; its content evaluates to
   T while μ is denied truth. The bill is paid deliberately.
4. **Paradoxes are extinguished by the quarantine flag, not by the
   tables**: ¬ has no fixed point, the liar cannot be seated by the
   tables. Z-sentences are exempted from the Tarski schema (the demand
   v(sentence)=v(content) is not levied on them).

## The price list (MEASURED audit.py, 2026-07-10)

**Alive laws (12):** modus ponens (semantic), non-contradiction
¬(p∧¬p), transitivity of →, commutativity of ∧ and ∨, associativity of
∧ and ∨, both distributivities, and the three canonical definitions:
p→q = ¬p∨q, p⊕q = (p∧¬q)∨(¬p∧q), p↔q = (p∧q)∨(¬p∧¬q) = (p→q)∧(q→p).

**Fallen laws (14) — a manifesto, not an apology:** double negation,
both De Morgan laws, contraposition, ⊕=¬(↔), idempotence of ∧ and ∨,
absorption, the units (p∧T=p, p∨F=p), excluded middle, reflexivity
p→p, Peirce's law, affirmation of the consequent q→(p→q).
Common trait of the fallen: all break only on Z, and all are laws that
grant truth "for free" (via polarity flip or tautologousness of form).

**Greediness theorem (MEASURED):** no compound formula ever takes the
value Z; the whole system is generated from {¬,∧,∨} (→, ⊕, ↔ are
definable by the surviving identities above).

Important: **modus ponens is ALIVE** (T→Z=F blocks the counterexample) —
ZTL is stronger in inference than Priest's LP, where MP falls.

## Place on the map (literature; checked 2026-07-10 against
Karpenko–Tomova 2017)

* Home: **Bochvar's B3 (1938)**, the external fragment. ¬, ∧, ∨
  coincide cell-for-cell with his ⌉, ∩□, ∪□. All ZTL connectives are
  external functions ⇒ functionally ZTL embeds in B3; there are no new
  functions (there never are — Finn).
* The delta: **7 cells** in →, ↔, ⊕ (Z→Z, Z→F, Z↔Z, Z↔F, F↔Z, Z⊕T,
  T⊕Z) — a systematic closing of the places where Bochvar's quarantine
  grants vacuous truth. ZTL's implication = his ◇A ⊃ □B, taken as a
  primitive.
* ZTL **falls outside Tomova's classification of "natural
  implications"** (it fails reflexivity p→p on Z) — a signature of
  novelty and, simultaneously, the first expected objection from
  logicians.
* Kindred in spirit: supervaluationism (rigid, non-tabular), SQL NULL
  (lazy), IEEE NaN (comparisons fail), exceptions with try/catch at
  every node (greediness). The motivation "greedy local supervaluation
  / default deny" was not found in the literature — not found ≠ does
  not exist.

## The keel: the entailment relation (MEASURED entailment.py,
2026-07-10)

Γ ⊨ φ ⟺ every valuation making all premises T makes the conclusion T
(only {T} is designated). Tarskian by construction: reflexivity,
monotonicity, cut follow from the form of the definition.

**The main discovery of this stage — the rules-versus-laws split:**
classical logic cannot tell them apart (the deduction theorem glues
them), ZTL separates them sharply. Of 14 classical inference rules,
**12** survive, including contraposition-as-a-rule (p→q ⊨ ¬q→¬p — even
though the identity law fell!), the K-rule (q ⊨ p→q), explosion and
resolution. Two fall: ¬¬-elimination (¬¬p ⊨ p, counterexample p=Z — Z
leaks through double negation) and "tautology in the conclusion"
(p ⊨ q∨¬q — a fresh atom in the conclusion earns no truth).

**The deduction theorem — left-to-right only**: ⊨A→B implies A⊨B (that
is MP), but A⊨B does NOT imply ⊨A→B (p⊨p, yet ⊭p→p). The arrow is
stricter than entailment — it does not internalize ⊨; it is a verdict
that must be earned. Consequence for the calculus stage: build with
sequents (rules), not axioms (laws).

**Terminological honesty:** ZTL is **paracomplete** (gaps exist, LEM
fell), but NOT paraconsistent in the ⊨ sense: v(p)=v(¬p)=T is
unattainable, there are no gluts, explosion is vacuously valid. The
liar is extinguished by quarantine (non-assertion), not by tolerance of
contradiction.
