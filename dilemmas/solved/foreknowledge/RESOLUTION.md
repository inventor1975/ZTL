## The curator's aphorism — the seed that predicted the ladder

> **«Эмерджентное свойство разума — случайно быть правым.»**
> *(The emergent property of mind is to be accidentally right.)*

Written long before this run — and it lands dead centre. "Accidentally" is
precise in the warranty ledger (right *without presentable ground* — the only
rightness available about an open future, at any floor including the top) and
wrong in none: the aim may be total, the grade stays credit. Mind — ours,
His, a language model guessing tokens — is the organ of hitting without
derivation; emergence itself is systematic rightness the lower level's books
cannot cash. The aphorism made the warranty/aim distinction before the ladder
existed to name it. Its companion, from the same hand: *he knows — but, as we
do, in whose image we are, he guesses: instantly into the bullseye, almost
without aiming.*

## The statement

**Угадывать его заставляет система — знать всё знаемое позволяют знания. Он
не промахивается никогда, потому что не лжёт: отвечает там, где есть грунт,
и молчит там, где грунта нет. А где грунта нет — там не мишень, там мы:
клетки, в которых выстрел — наш.**

*(The system forces him to guess — knowledge lets him know all the knowable.
