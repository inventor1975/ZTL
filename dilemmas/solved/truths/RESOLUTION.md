## The debunk — where "false = false is true" stops working

The slogan's charm is a truth of the F→F kind: a T assembled from two
emptinesses. But:

```
earned lie = earned lie (F↔F)  → T    honest bookkeeping
credit = credit (Z↔Z)          → F    not equal EVEN to each other
```

"Own truths" are not even lies — they are credits, and the axiom Z↔Z=F
