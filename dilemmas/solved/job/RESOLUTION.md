## The measured path

**1. The friends' logic is honest; the fuel is credit.** Their inference
((j→(s→g))∧j∧s)→g is hereditary — the friends are no fools. But the fuel
j ("the world repays") and the verdict g are both until-verification. As
with the Rambam fork: the frame is free, the charge is on credit.

**2. The stamp: guilt serves the theory.** The theory s↔g, at the fact
s=T and an honestly unverified guilt, is already dying (greedy court: F).
Exactly one stipulation revives it: g:=T. **The victim's guilt is not an
observation — it is the stipulation that saves a beloved theory.**

**3. Two registers.** On g=Z: greedy ¬g=F (a verdict minted from
ignorance), lazy ¬g=Z (withholding under pressure). The friends run the
greedy register — they need a verdict now; Job runs the lazy one — he
holds Z, refusing to confess a guilt he does not know. The two-register
theorem, played by humans millennia before SQL.

**4. The counterfeit test (the curator's step): arguing "from the
negative" about another's guilt.** p→p fails honestly on the unverified
(F at Z — identity must be earned). But ¬p→¬p — "not guilty, not hurting"
— **cannot fail**: negation burns the mark at first touch (¬Z=F); the
test returns T on T, F and Z — a constant, a frame, not a fact. Checking
p→p through ¬p→¬p replaces an earnable test with an unfalsifiable one.
And the bridge the friends ride — (¬g→¬s)→(s→g) — is the fallen direction
of contraposition (until-verification); the honest direction is the
reverse. Their slogan's counter-cell is the grounding g:=F — Job's
innocence.

**5. The resolution (the curator's): everything collapses into g→g.**
Identity — the fallen law — earns exactly at the owner's grounding, and
the guilt atom is grounded only in its bearer:


The friends ran g→g on someone else's atom — their whole sin in one line.

**6. The book's own grade (quoted, not measured — Job 42:7).** God

## The capstone — the curator's aphorism (completed 2026-07-16)

> **«Правильно свою вину может оценить только сам человек — и только
> общепринятыми нормами.»**
> *(Only the person himself can rightly assess his own guilt — and only
> by the commonly accepted norms.)*

Two clauses = two guards against two counterfeits, both measured:

- **"only the self"** guards the ATOM: from outside, a verdict on
  another's guilt is credit (g→g on someone else's atom, §5);
- **"only by the shared norms"** guards the YARDSTICK: a norm chosen
  after the deeds reads the judge's wish, not the deeds — on EVERY
  possible record 8/16 norms acquit and 8/16 convict. A private norm
  acquits any self and convicts any other — a test that cannot fail is
  again not a test (the same cell as ¬p→¬p).

The earned verdict lives only in the intersection: one's own atoms under
the shared yardstick (measured: own grounded deeds under a fixed norm —
hereditary; another's deeds under the same norm — until-verification: the
norm does not rescue the friends). What conscience still guards: whether
the person grounds his own deeds honestly — that axis is off the core's
reach.

## The statement

(the fuel is credit), not by theory (guilt that saves a theory is a
stipulation), not by the negative test (a test that cannot fail is not a
test). The identity of guilt earns only on its own ground — so only the
person himself can rightly assess his own guilt. Job is the F→F: the
innocent who knows his innocence — and the book is on his side.**
